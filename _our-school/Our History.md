<h1>OUR HISTORY</h1>
<p><strong>(1977 - PRESENT)</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/H1.png">
</div>
<p><em>Boon Lay Garden Primary School campus during the 1970s.</em>
</p>
<p>The school was established in 1977 along Boon Lay Avenue with an enrollment
of 1080 students in 27 classes. It was declared open officially by Mr Ngeow
Pack Hua, then Member of Parliament for Boon Lay, on 18 August 1978.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/H2.jpg">
</div>
<p><em>The original wall plaque of the official opening of our first campus in 1978.</em>
</p>
<p>We shifted to our current campus in 2001 and had the honour of having
Mdm Ho Geok Choo, then Member of Parliament for West Coast GRC, to officiate
the opening on 31 July 2004.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:100%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/H3.png">
</div>
<p><em>The original acrylic wall plaque for the official opening of our current campus and a model of BLGPS.</em>
</p>
<p>In its years of existence, the school has been under the caring leadership
of seven principals. The present Principal is Mrs Christine Low and the
Vice Principals are Mdm Ng Boon In, Mrs Avinash Murugesuvari and Mr Dennis
Oh.</p>
